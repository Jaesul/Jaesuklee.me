About Me

I’m a UX designer and software, studying at the University of Washington.

UX and software is my way of helping those in need. I’ve always been focused on two things, people and technology. UX brings together these two passions by focusing first on how to meet the needs of users through empathy, and second how to utilize technology to meet those needs.
